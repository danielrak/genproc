# genproc (development version)

* Package skeleton initialized. No user-facing functionality yet.
* Scope for the first release (v0.1): mandatory *logged* and
  *reproducibility* layers around a `genproc()` runner, plus the four
  meta-functions ported from `genprocShiny`
  (`from_example_to_function()`, `from_function_to_mask()`,
  `rename_function_params()`, `add_trycatch_logrow()`).
